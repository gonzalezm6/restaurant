About Us
As a family owned and operated restaurant, Forchetta takes great pride in who we are and what we do. Our commitment to quality is unrivaled in the city of Burbank. Whether we are slicing up fresh vegetables for use in our family recipes or hand pulling pizza dough each morning, you will always get a fresh product each and every time you visit. Having grown up in the North End of Boston, we have brought truly authentic East Coast recipes, methods, and style to sunny California for your family to enjoy!
